Steps

Tzatziki

  1. Grate half a large cucumber into a medium sized bowl
  2. Squeeze out water from cucumber shreds and remove liquid
  3. Add a cup of greek yogurt to the bowl
  4. Squeeze 1 lemon of juice into bowl
  5. Add 1 tbsp of dill
  6. Add 1 tbsp of mint
  7. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic
  8. ADd 1 tbsp of olive oil

Meat

  1. Cut top round steak into thin strips
  2. Cook on medium heat
  3. Added a gracious amount of worchester and soy sauce
  4. Sprinkle a gracious amount of garlic powder
  5. Cook till fully brown

Gyro

  1. Place strips of meat on naan
  2. Cover with Tzatziki sauce
  3. Sprinkle feta cheese
