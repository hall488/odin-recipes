Pizza


Description

This is a homemade pizza my family has been making for generations! My grandmother snuck this pizza recipe up her butt and brought it to America during WW2. She was coming from Canada...


Ingredients

  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Water
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Minced Garlic
  • Mozerella
  • Parmesean
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushroom

Steps

Pizza Dough

  1. Microwave 1.75 cups of water until warm
  2. Place water in mixer bowl
  3. Add 2.25 tsp of yeast to bowl
  4. Let sit for 10 minutes, allowing for yeast to bloom
  5. Add 2 tbsp of olive oil to bowl
  6. Add 2 cups of flour to bowl
  7. Add .5 tsp of salt to bowl
  8. Mix dough and add 2 more cups of flour while mixing
  9. Put oven on warm or preheat to 200 degrees F
  10. Turn of mixer and knead dough for 10 minutes
  11. Coat mixing bowl in olive oil
  12. Ball the dough and place back into the bowl
  13. Turn off oven if it was preheated to 200 degrees F
  14. Place warm damp cloth over bowl
  15. Let dough rise in oven for 1 hour or longer

Pizza Sauce

  1. Place 1 can of tomato sauce in medium sized bowl
  2. Mix in 1 small can of tomato paste
  3. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil
  4. Add 1 tbsp of italian seasoning
  5. Add 3 gloves of minced garlic
  6. Mix well and set aside

Pizza!

  1. Remove dough from oven and cut into halves
  2. Save half of dough for other pizza
  3. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  4. Knead dough for 5 minutes
  5. Roll to shape of pizza pan
  6. Place in pan and coat botttom with pizza sauce
  7. Place a horendous amount of Mozerella over the sauce
  8. Shred parmesean over pizza
  9. Cut up several mushrooms into thin slices
  10. Place pepperoni and mushroom onto pizza
  11. Place in oven for 15-20 minutes
